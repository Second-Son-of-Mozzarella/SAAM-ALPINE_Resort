Welcome

Step into the past when you visit the Stanley hotel. We have been serving guests since 1824 and have been dedicated to providing a luxury experience and authentic Swiss culture. This hotel has been managed by the same family for all this time and we hope you enjoy your stay.

Commonly Asked Questions

Do we allow pets? No we do not allow pets unfortunately bet we would be happy to provide you with numbers to some local animal housing areas while you enjoy your stay.

Can we drive in? No the only way to reach the hotel is to take a train from astrid station that stops at the hotel 6 times a day. You can see times listed here.

Are you able to get to the local town by walking? YES! In fact the train will drop you off in the city and the hotel will be only a short walk to the edge of the village.

Is breakfast served? Breakfast is served from 6am to 8am daily
